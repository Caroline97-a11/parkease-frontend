ParkEase Parking Management System

ParkEase is a vehicle parking management system is designed to help parking attendants and administrators efficiently manage parking operations. 
The purpose of ParkEase is to simplify parking management while also providing additional vehicle support services such as battery hire, tyre clinic within a parking facility.

Features
User login - users login to the system and access the different dashboards based on their roles
Vehicle Registration – Register vehicles entering the parking area.
Search Functionality – Search receipts using Receipt ID or Plate Number.
Receipt Generation – Automatically generate parking receipts.
Battery Service – Provide assistance for vehicles with battery problems.
Tyre Clinic – Support for tyre repair and tyre-related services.
Daily Reports – Track parking and service activities.
